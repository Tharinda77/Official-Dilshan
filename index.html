ABOUT ME

Hey, I'm Tharinda Dilshan

Hey there! I'm Tharinda Dilshan, a 22-year-old graphic designer, video editor, VFX GFX editor, and Graphic designer residing in the serene town of Hanwella, Sri Lanka. And yes, I'm still flying solo!

Born and raised in Sri Lanka, I've always had a knack for creativity and design. From doodling in my school notebooks to crafting digital masterpieces, my passion for visual arts has only grown stronger over the years.

Read More

Currently, I proudly serve as the Co-Founder of Transition's Studio, a dynamic creative agency dedicated to bringing ideas to life through innovative design and multimedia solutions. Additionally, I'm part of the talented team at Tos Lanka Co-Pvt Ltd, where I contribute my skills and expertise to exciting projects.

As a multi-disciplinary creative, I specialize in graphic design, video editing, VFX GFX editing, and Graphic design. I thrive on transforming concepts into captivating visuals and immersive digital experiences that leave a lasting impression.

Beyond pixels and pixels, I'm fueled by my love for hard work, music, and the relentless pursuit of excellence. I believe in the power of creativity to transcend boundaries, evoke emotions, and inspire change.

Ready to bring your vision to life? Don't hesitate to reach out! You can contact me at dilshantharinda610@gmail. com or tharindadilshaninfo@gmail.com. Let's connect on Facebook too: Tharinda Dilshan. Thank you for stopping by my portfolio site—I appreciate your visit!

Graphic Design

Graphic design is the process of visually communicating ideas and information through images, typography, and layout. It's a creative field that combines art and technology to inform, inspire, and captivate audiences, playing a crucial role in various industries. Essentially, graphic designers use visual elements to convey specific messages and achieve particular objectives.

Video Editing

Video editing is the process of assembling and manipulating video and audio to create a final, polished product. It involves selecting, arranging, and refining video clips, adding transitions, effects, and sound to enhance the narrative and visual experience. Ultimately, video editing shapes raw footage into a compelling and cohesive story.
